How it works

1. Enter your email address (same one you use for CSV uploads)

2. Add menu items and map them to ingredients (e.g., Latte ‚Üí 8oz milk)

3. Save your mappings - they'll be used automatically when you upload CSVs

4. You can add multiple ingredients per menu item (e.g., Mocha ‚Üí 8oz milk + 2oz chocolate)
